Foodie AI

Gemini USDA Meal diary

Foodie AI is a Progressive Web App that uses Gemini vision to read your plate and estimate portion size, then pulls verified nutrient data from USDA FoodData Central (per 100 g). Calories, protein, fat, carbs, and fiber for your portion are computed locally; you can switch display units between grams, ounces, and pounds without another API call. A lightweight on-device diary keeps thumbnails and scan metadata from recent meals.
