BP_Chart Visualization Workspace

Iterative design workspace for optimizing blood pressure and pulse visualizations.

This page serves as a sandbox to experiment with Chart.js configurations to achieve the optimal "Small Multiples" and "LOWESS Smoothing" visualizations described in the design research.

1. Daily View (Donut)

Goal: Immediate clarity on the proportion of readings within target ranges.

2. Weekly View (Multi-Axis Line)

Goal: Spotting short-term trends and correlations between BP and Pulse.

3. Monthly View (Stacked Bar)

Goal: Categorization of reading status over longer periods.

4. Monthly View (Bubble)

Goal: Exploring time-of-day impact on readings.

5. Yearly View (Area)

Goal: Long-term progression and envelope analysis.
